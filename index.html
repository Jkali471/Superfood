Superfoods, Vitamins, Minerals, and Supplements

Blueberries

Blueberries are high in antioxidants and vitamin C, which can help boost the immune system and prevent cellular damage. It is recommended to consume 1/2 to 1 cup of blueberries daily.

Spinach

Spinach is rich in iron, vitamin K, and calcium, which can help improve bone health and blood circulation. It is recommended to consume 1 cup of spinach daily.

Almonds

Almonds are high in healthy fats, fiber, and protein, which can help reduce the risk of heart disease and promote weight loss. It is recommended to consume 1 ounce or 23 almonds daily.
