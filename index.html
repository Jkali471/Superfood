Superfoods, Vitamins, Minerals, and Supplements

Here are 20 superfoods, vitamins, minerals, and supplements that can help you optimize your health and fitness:

  • Spinach: Rich in iron, calcium, and vitamin K. Add to smoothies, salads, or omelets. Aim for 1-2 cups daily.
  • Blueberries: High in antioxidants, vitamin C, and fiber. Enjoy as a snack or add to smoothies or yogurt. Aim for 1 cup daily.
  • Salmon: Packed with omega-3 fatty acids, protein, and vitamin D. Bake, grill, or broil for a healthy dinner. Aim for 2 servings per week.
  • Green Tea: Loaded with antioxidants and a natural source of caffeine. Drink hot or iced. Aim for 1-2 cups daily.
  • Almonds: Rich in healthy fats, fiber, and protein. Snack on a handful or add to salads or oatmeal. Aim for 1/4 cup daily.
  • Quinoa: A great source of protein, fiber, and iron. Use in place of rice or pasta in meals. Aim for 1/2 to 1 cup cooked daily.
  • Turmeric: Contains curcumin, a natural anti-inflammatory. Use in curries, soups, or smoothies. Aim for 1 teaspoon daily.
  • Garlic: Contains allicin, which has immune-boosting properties. Add to sauces, soups, or roasted vegetables. Aim for 1-2 cloves daily.
  • Probiotics: Good bacteria that can help improve digestion
